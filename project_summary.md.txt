# Healthcare Analytics & Revenue Intelligence Platform

## Executive Summary

Developed a cloud-based healthcare analytics solution using Supabase PostgreSQL and SQL to transform raw patient admission data into a structured star-schema data warehouse.

The project processes 55,500 healthcare admission records and enables business intelligence reporting across patient demographics, hospital performance, insurance utilization, revenue generation, and operational metrics.

---

## Business Problem

Healthcare organizations generate large amounts of operational and financial data.

Without structured analytics, decision-makers struggle to answer questions such as:

- Which insurance providers contribute the most revenue?
- Which hospitals generate the highest billing amounts?
- What are the monthly admission trends?
- Which age groups require the most healthcare resources?
- What is the average patient length of stay?

---

## Solution

A cloud-based analytical data warehouse was designed using PostgreSQL on Supabase.

The solution follows a dimensional modeling approach:

### Staging Layer
- stg_healthcare

### Dimension Tables
- dim_patient
- dim_doctor
- dim_hospital
- dim_insurance
- dim_date

### Fact Table
- fact_admissions

---

## ETL Workflow

1. Load raw healthcare records into staging.
2. Extract unique business entities into dimension tables.
3. Create date dimension for time-based analysis.
4. Populate fact table with foreign-key relationships.
5. Generate derived metrics such as Length of Stay.
